Meet your dentist

Dr. H Mtshali, BDS

Family & Cosmetic Dentistry

With over a decade of clinical experience, Dr. H Mtshali earned his BDS from the University of the Witwatersrand and has devoted his career to combining modern techniques with a truly gentle, unhurried approach.

He believes exceptional dentistry starts with listening — understanding your concerns, explaining every option clearly, and building a plan around your comfort. His focus on preventive care means fewer surprises and healthier smiles for the whole family.

Whether you're bringing your child in for their first visit or ready for a full smile makeover, you'll be treated with warmth, honesty, and the highest standard of care.

What we offer

Comprehensive dental care, all in one place

From routine cleanings to full smile makeovers, we care for every generation of your family.

General Checkups & Cleanings

Routine exams and professional cleanings to keep your smile healthy year-round.

Teeth Whitening

Safe, in-chair whitening that brightens your smile several shades in a single visit.

Dental Fillings

Tooth-coloured composite fillings that restore decayed teeth and blend seamlessly.

Root Canal Therapy

Modern, comfortable root canal treatment to save infected teeth without pain.

Tooth Extractions

Gentle removal of damaged teeth with sedation options for anxious patients.

Crowns & Bridges

Custom porcelain crowns and bridges that restore strength and natural appearance.

Dentures

Comfortable full and partial dentures crafted for a natural, confident smile.

Veneers

Ultra-thin porcelain veneers for a flawless, red-carpet smile transformation.

Orthodontics / Braces

Traditional and ceramic braces to straighten teeth and correct bite issues.

Invisalign / Clear Aligners

Nearly invisible aligners that straighten your teeth without metal brackets.

Pediatric Dentistry

Fun, gentle dental care that helps children build lifelong healthy habits.

Dental Implants

Permanent titanium implants that look, feel, and function like natural teeth.

Gum Disease Treatment

Periodontal therapy to treat gum disease and protect your long-term oral health.

Wisdom Tooth Removal

Safe, minimally invasive removal of wisdom teeth with rapid recovery care.

Emergency Dental Care

Same-day emergency slots for toothaches, breaks, and dental trauma.

Oral Cancer Screening

Thorough screening at every checkup for early detection and peace of mind.

Sedation Dentistry

Relaxing sedation options for nervous patients or longer procedures.

Cosmetic Bonding

Quick, affordable bonding to repair chips, gaps, and reshape teeth in one visit.
